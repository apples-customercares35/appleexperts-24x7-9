3 WAYS TO CLEAN

What's the difference between a Full Service Car Wash, an Express Car Wash, and Detailing?

At Thomas Car Wash we offer so many options to make sure your wash ends with great results. Learn the difference between our Express Wash, Full Service Wash, and Detailing. Looking for a touchfree carwash? We have that too! That's our new Clementine Carwash!

Express Wash
Full Service Wash
Detailing

1.

EXTERIOR EXPRESS CAR WASH

Currently the most popular style for new car wash builds in the United States, the express exterior model utilizes a conveyor which carries the vehicle and its occupants through a long building (called a tunnel) as automated equipment does the washing. In the tunnel wash, you stay in your vehicle as it's brought through the wash to complete the wash process. After the wash, the driver can access our FREE vacuum stations to continue cleaning their vehicle the way they like.

  • RESTRICTED VEHICLES:

    • Vehicles taller than 7ft, 2in
    • Tires wider than 12" 
    • Double-wide tires (Duallys)
    • Cars with bike racks on
    • Cars with wheelchair lifts
    • Cars lower than without a 6 inch clearance 
    • Trucks that have any items in the truck bed 

2.

FULL SERVICE CAR WASH

At full-service car washes, the exterior of the car is washed mechanically, with attendants available to dry the car manually and to clean the interior.  In the tunnel wash, your vehicle is lifted onto a conveyer belt to bring your car through the wash. After the soft touch tunnel wash our crew will follow-up with an interior vacuum, dusting of the console, dash and cup holders. The experience is finished off with a hand towel dry.

  • RESTRICTED VEHICLES:

    • Vehicles taller than 7ft, 2in
    • Tires wider than 12" 
    • Double-wide tires (Duallys)
    • Cars with bike racks on
    • Cars with wheelchair lifts
    • Cars lower than without a 6 inch clearance 
    • Trucks that have any items in the truck bed 

3.

DETAILING

Because we want to serve our customers with exactly what they need for their vehicle, we also provide express and full detail services. Whether you’re looking for a carpet shampoo, seat cleaning or synthetic sealer wax, we’ve got you covered. Not only do we offer great deals on our full detail services, but we make it easy to save by bundling multiple interior and exterior vehicle cleaning services.
